The gallery

A calm, premium space for art inspired by place, texture, and story.

Our gallery brings together selected original artworks, including pieces by selected local artists and other local artists. The focus is not mass-produced décor, but meaningful works that carry atmosphere, craft, and a sense of the Karoo.
