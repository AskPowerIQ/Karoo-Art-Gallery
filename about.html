About

A gallery shaped by the Karoo

KAROO ART GALLERY exists to create a calm, premium space where travellers and local visitors can discover original artworks in person.

More than a catalogue — a physical gallery experience.

The gallery showcases work by Lize Smith and other selected artists. Our focus is on original pieces, local character, texture, atmosphere, and art that feels meaningful in a home or collected space.
